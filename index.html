DIFFERENT.

Forty Four was born from a simple obsession: making Munich's best burger. Not the most complicated. Not the trendiest. The best.

Every patty is sourced from regional, predominantly organic farms. Every bun arrives fresh daily. Every sauce is made in-house. There are no shortcuts here.

We opened our doors in October 2023 at Blumenstraße 25 in the heart of Munich's Altstadt. Nearly 1,000 reviews and a 4.8 rating later, the city seems to agree.
